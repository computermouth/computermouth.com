Donate

Well hey, thanks for your interest!

If you feel like making a donation, processing is done through Stripe. All transactions are fully encrypted by SSL/TLS (just check your browsers address bar for the little lock) and email reciepts are automatic. No information will remain on this server. All personal information is forwarded directly to Stripe's API.

Source code for this payment service is also available on Github. (spoiler alert: it's just fcgi and libcurl)

NOTE: donation is taken in WHOLE DOLLAR AMOUNTS, as denoted by the [$][ . . . ][.00] format of the 'Amount' input box.
