Info

I love Linux, I love programming, and I love computer hardware. Most of my professional career has been in PC support, maintenance, and administration. I've been programming since 2004, starting with pre-.NET Visual Basic, and later moving to C/C++ and dabbling in some scripting/markup languages along the way. In 2008 I was introduced to Ubuntu Linux for a project that required the manipulation of local system specs. Soon I tried every distro I could get my hands on before finding my home on Debian.
